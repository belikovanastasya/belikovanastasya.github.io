Sketch 1

Long jacket + trousers

Yevgeniya Ugors’ka

Dolyna, Ukraine

As a child Zhenia wanted to sew clothes. She saw her grandmother sewing and that was fascinating. You cannot even imagine how many mom’s dresses were spoiled by the talented girl. For 8 years she has been making clothes at home but still has a dream to become a fashion designer. In 2018 she finished professional courses and got caught up even more. Now she strives to move only in this direction.

The designer was inspired by Tencent Seafront Towers that was built in Shenzhen, China in 2017. Only thirty years ago this city was a small fishing port. Now it is an important industrial, financial and transport center.

In this way the designer saw the mood of Shenzhen, the city of skyscrapers, natural parks and industrial giants. In her view in order to differentiate you need to choose original looks even for daily clothes today. Asymmetry is what makes this model original and bold enough.

The designer suggests to make the long jacket of grey fabric with Scottish pattern. For trousers she recommends brown velvet.

To make the model more interesting the designer has added velvet stripe on shoulders, patches on the elbows and a belt of the same fabric.
