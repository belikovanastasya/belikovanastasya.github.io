PARTICIPATE
Register. Create your own profile, fill in the documents required and attach your sketches in accordance with the criteria of the project.

Monitor voting and discussion
The sketches selected according to the criteria appear on our creative ATTACH board. The community joins and starts commenting and voting.

GET TO THE FINAL
The sketches that receive the most votes get to the final. Our experts make prototypes and calculate the cost of production and materials.

GET YOUR PROFIT
You get profit share of items sold. The models produced will be added to your portfolio on the Attach platform and announced.
