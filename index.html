FAQ

Q: What if my luggage measurements exceed the dimentions listed on this site?
A: It is strongly advised against stuffing and overloading the locker cell. The door should close freely. The locking mechanism, although robust, potentially can be jammed and you might have difficulties opening the locker.
Q: I’m having difficulties opening the locker?
A: Most likely, you put too many bags in one locker, or the luggage is too large for the cell. This pushes the door outwards and makes it difficult for the lock to release the door. Try applying pressure to the door to help the lock to unlatch. If that doesn’t help, please call the Technical Support +371 24 603 006
Q: Can I pick up my luggage during non-working hours?
A: If the front door is locked, you can still access Keep My Bag. Find the keypad at the entrance and dial the code 2510#
Q: How safe is my luggage?
A: Keep My Bag premesis is monitored by CCTV. Also the building entrance is closed for non-working hours for extra security.
Q: Can I put several items in a single locker?
A: There is no restriction on the number of items in a locker. However, please make sure that the locker door can be closed freely without pressing on the door from inside.
Q: How should I plan my time to pick up my luggage?
A: Picking up your luggage should be quick, easy and reliable. Nevertheless, just be on the safe side, please plan your time responsibly and extra 30 minutes for picking up your luggage from the locker.
