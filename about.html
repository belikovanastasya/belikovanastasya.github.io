Attach Philosophy

ATTACH is a co-creation platform where you can:

  • Try yourself as a designer and turn your sketches into clothes without financial investment;
  • Buy unique individually tailored clothes and be the first to discover new talents in fashion world.

We give high priority to creativity and stimulate new and original ideas. We crowdsource cool fashion sketches and engage the community to transform them into reality. Each project that we announce is a new collection that we co-create.

Joining Attach community as a designer, you get a chance to share ideas and get feedback. We involve consumers to have a dialogue. Сo-creation gives you all sorts of applicable insights in real time on what your potential customers are looking for.

As a consumer, you will also appreciate the dialogue, which will likely result in clothes you want to wear. You can be a member of our virtual “Buyers” team telling which designs you love. You get more chances to buy your favourites in the final by sharing them and voting.

Whether you want to watch or get involved in creative process, you need to register. Once you are logged in, you will have access to ATTACH community and will be able to find out more about our co-creation projects. Regardless of whether you shared an idea or not, you will be able to vote, comment and buy clothes.

We try to develop collaborative culture and shared creativity to provide more chances for emerging talents that may not have knowledge or finance to go at it alone. Engaging the consumers, we give them chance to participate in creative process with their own tastes and commit to new talents discovery.
