Sketch 2. Breakfast at Tiffany's

Jacket-dress + golf-dress

Valentyna Tymchyk

Odessa, Ukraine

Valentyna has started to study fashion design just recently and is a student now. But she has been involved in fashion for a long time drawing sketches and sewing clothes to order. Now she has been improving her skills in clothes design and modelling.

The designer found inspiration in the lovely style and atmosphere of the film that girls of all generations adore - “Breakfast at Tiffany's”. She has reflected incomparable charm of the great actress Hepburn Audrey – her delicacy, aristocratic manners and elegance have formed the basis for new model.

The author of this sketch associates classic Scottish pattern fabric with aristocratic luxury from the one side and with warmth, comfort, winter and traditions from the other side.

As a result, we see this model warm and luxury. You can wear the jacket-dress with a waist belt over the knitted golf-dress or use them separately.

The designer suggests to combine a strict woollen Scottish pattern fabric with a soft lurex shiny knitting that will emphasize the modern style of the model.
