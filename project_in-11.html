Sketch 11. “Dovima with Elephants”

Dress

Marta Klymkevych

Lviv, Ukraine

Marta is a fashion designer. For 11 years, she was engaged in visual advertising, but a lifelong dream didn’t leave her. All the time she thought about clothes design and drew sketches. Several years ago she got a chance to try her hand in fashion industry. Marta got caught up in the process so much that she still cannot stop. Last year her work «Kiev meets Berlin» won a competition. Marta designed costumes for Christmas fair organized on the square in front of Charlottenburg castle in Berlin.

«Dovima with elephants » is one of the most famous photos in the world. It was made in the 1950s for advertising campaign of a new collection of Christian Dior “Autumn- Winter, 1955”.

The photo shoot took place in Paris in the building of the circus. For the first time the photo was printed in the famous fashion magazine Harper’s Bazaar. The elegant black dress was the first work of an unknown 19-year-old designer who was Dior’s assistant at that time. The name of that young man was Yves Saint Laurent.

It was the photo mentioned above that inspired our designer to create this evening dress made of black stretch velvet and natural silk of a light colour. Nice silver earrings decorated with ostrich feathers make the look complete.
