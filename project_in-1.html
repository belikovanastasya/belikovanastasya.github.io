Sketch 1

Pantsuit + Blouse

Darya Shuvayeva

Lysychans’k, Lugansk Region, Ukraine

When Dasha was a child she liked painting and sewing clothes for her dolls most of all. At age six she asked her mother, skilled in tailoring, to teach her to sew too. After leaving school she started studying graphic design and in the fifth year became an artist of computer games. But she still thinks about fashion design and new models often come to her head. She enjoys drawing fashion sketches.

The author of this idea was inspired by business dress code of the 1980s. Looking through Yves Saint Laurent’s designs of that period she got a desire to create an image of a successful woman who is strong and tender at the same time. Loose-fit high-waisted trousers make the look both reserved and female.

To make the image lighter and to add originality to the design the author has added a short waistcoat with deflated shoulder straps decorated with folds near the armhole. The wide belt covered with cloth, as an echo of the 80s trends, emphasizes the waist and the blouse’s puffy sleeves make a hourglass figure type.

The designer wants to use soft natural fabrics in calm colours for this suit. She suggests delicate cream silk for the blouse, and soft woolen fabric of grey colour for the trousers and waistcoat.
