Sketch 5

Пальто + брюки

Anastasiya Litovchenko

Kyiv, Ukraine

Nastia is a student of «Clothes and Accessories Design Department” of Kyiv National University of Culture and Arts. It is her childhood dream to become a fashion designer. It is already the second time when she participates in our projects.

While creating this model the author was inspired by her impressions from Melbourne, Australia. The model shows freedom, relaxed and groovy mood of the city that is not running for fame and money, but tries its best to enjoy every minute and live for pleasure.

In 2017 The Economist determined Мельбурн as the best city for life giving it 97,5 points from 100 possible. In the same way as the city combines old and new, the model combines romantic, elegant and sports styles.

The designer suggests to use lacquer fabric for the coat to show its form well, and painted jeans cotton for the trousers.
