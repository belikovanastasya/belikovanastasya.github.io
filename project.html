What to Present? When submitting to the ROOY x PARSONS Design Challenge keep in mind that your submission will be evaluated on multiple dimensions; i.e. your design is considered together with its visual appeal, overall design, realization techniques and material of choice. Applicants should send high-resolution images of the design, together with a presentation (visual/mood board).

No Entry Fees The design challenge is completely free and students will not be asked to make any further fees for winning the award and everything listed in the winners benefits will be provided free of charge.

Submissions will be accepted from February 20 to March 24, 2017 with 1st-place winner receiving $2,000 along with the opportunity to develop their shoe and take it to market. 2nd place will receive $1,500 and 3rd place winner will receive $1,000. Finalists will be selected by community voting with the grand prize winner chosen by panel of judges from ROOY and Parsons. Students are encouraged to submit mood boards, inspiration pics, alternative colorways or multiple views of their sneaker concept.

SUBMISSION GUIDE

Image with 800 x 600 pixel w/cover image (JPG, PNG). Download Design Template

JUDGING CRITERIA
  • Attach Community Voting
  • Jasonpaul Mccarthy - Director of AAS Fashion Design and AAS Fashion Marketing
  • Francesca Sammaritano - Associate Director of AAS Fashion Design

When submitting your ideas keep in mind that they will be evaluated on multiple dimensions. Pay attention to visual appeal as well as to realization techniques.

Make sure to include:

  • Visual mood board (high-resolution image).
  • Design sketches (you can show alternative colorways if you want, high-resolution images).
  • Technical drawings (high-resolution images).
  • Written description (try to point to all the individual characteristics that make your design special and show us how it expresses the project theme).
  • Your suggestion on materials (images with specification showing where exactly each type of material is supposed to be applied).

ATTACH team will check all the submissions according to the requirements and give you feedback.

You don’t need to pay any application fees. Participation in the project is completely free. You can ATTACH your designs from August 20 to September 20, 2018. Finalists will be selected by community voting.

Before you start submitting your ideas you will need to create your profile and download all the files needed to complete the tasks.

It is important that you understand the legal aspects of the project and accept our Terms and Conditions. You assign all the rights to your idea, as well as permission to use your image and name to ATTACH. This permission means we can guarantee the originality of all of your ideas and use your name and image as a designer in this project if your idea is selected.
