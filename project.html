Fashion is cyclical. It often looks back. Most trends never truly disappear; they simply take an extended vacation and return later. Mini from the 60s, bell bottoms from the 70s or oversize sweaters that your mom wore.

We start our Vintage Revival. Find your vintage favourite and revive it. Bring it up to date while keeping the original’s traits. You can choose a design created by a famous couturier decades ago, or just take out garment from your mother’s or grandmother’s wardrobe.

Stroll down memory lane and check out some of the cool ideas that are sure to assume a new identity in your design

Criteria of the collection Vintage Revivals:

  • Conformity (to the subject of the collection)
  • Utility (this is the collection of street fashion)
  • Humanity (without leather and fur)
  • Creativity (we appreciate cool ideas)

While evaluating sketches, we take into account different aspects – images quality, technical drawings accuracy, creativity and design concept.

To apply you need to send:

  • Moodboard (high quality image, JPG or PNG, 600х800 pixels).
  • Fashion sketches, you can show alternative colors if you want (high quality images, JPG or PNG, 600х800 pixels).
  • Technical drawings (high quality images, JPG or PNG, 600х800 pixels).
  • Concept (tell us the story of your model and specify the characteristics that make your design special).
  • Your suggestion on fabrics (images of fabrics and the scheme of materials placement).

We will check all the sketches and give you feedback.

You need to register and fill in the documents that we will e-mail you. We accept sketches starting from the 28th of September to 31st of October to attachboard@gmail.com.

We respect copyright policy and it is important for us to follow the legal aspects. We want to be sure that you are the author of the idea and to have the right to use your name as a designer.

The application submitted confirms that we can publish your ideas and use images.
